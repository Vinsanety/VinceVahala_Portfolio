I'm a UX Designer, UI Developer, and outdoor explorer who loves the mountains and environment. You can find me trail running, climbing, snowboarding, camping, and exploring in my free time...

...bet I'll beat you to the summit.

From a small industrial town in Indiana, I grew up playing any sport imaginable in traveling leagues, in the backyard, or on a friend's outdoor basketball hoop. I attended Indiana University in beautiful Bloomington, Indiana where I studied at the School of Public and Environmental Affairs, resulting in a B.S. in Environmental Management. I also studied at IU's Kelley School of Business, earning a minor in Marketing.


During my time at IU, I found the opportunity to travel abroad for a semester in Barcelona, Spain, studying Public Policy, the "Barcelona Model" of City Planning, Environmentalism, and Regional Recyclying and Composting programs. After graduation, I packed my Jeep up with all that would fit and moved to Colorado.


Upon arriving in Colorado, I worked in Sales, IT Recruiting, Marketing, the Service-Industry and other odd-jobs before finding my passion in Web Development and Software Engineering. To transition into this industry, I went back to school at Galvanize's Software Engineering Immersive, a full-stack 6-month coding bootcamp. This is where I fell in love with the problem-sovling and design challenges of being a Software Engineer. Since graduating from Galvanize, I have been working full-time delivering responsive web-development and software solutions to both small businesses as an independent developer as well as the companies that I have been employed at. I love working on collaborative teams, tinkering with the latest technologies, and always expanding my design chops through researching the latest UX Patterns and UI interactions.
