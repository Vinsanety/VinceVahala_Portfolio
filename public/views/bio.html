Vince Vahala

Hello! I'm Vince Vahala. Frontend Application Developer, UI/UX guru, CSS Ninja with a profound appreciation and childlike infatuation with mountains. Climbing, hiking, camping, and exploring in my free time. Guarantee I'll out-run you and don't even try me on the Ping Pong table.

From a small industrial town in Indiana, I grew up playing any sport imaginable in traveling leagues, in the backyard, or on a friend's outdoor baskeball hoop. I attended Indiana University in Beautiful Bloomington, IN, studying Environmental Management at IU's School of Public and Environmental Affairs as well as Marketing through the Kelley School of Business. While at school I studied abroad a semester in Barcelona, Catalonia, Espana. After graduation, I packed my Jeep up with all that would fit and moved to Colorado. Best decision of my life.
Currently I am an independent Frontend Developer and Podcast co-host for Spoilers! Podcast.
