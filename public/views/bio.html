UX Designer, UI Developer, and Outdoorsman with a profound appreciation for mountains and the environment. Trail running, climbing, snowboarding, camping, and exploring in my free time...

...bet I'll beat you to the summit.

From a small industrial town in Indiana, I grew up playing any sport imaginable in traveling leagues, in the backyard, or on a friend's outdoor basketball hoop. I attended Indiana University in beautiful Bloomington, IN, studying Environmental Management at the School of Public and Environmental Affairs, while also snagging a Marketing minor through the Kelley School of Business.


During my undergrad studies I found the opportunity to go abroad for a semester to study City Planning, Environmentalism, Public Policy, and the "Barcelona Model" in Barcelona, Spain. While abroad, I fell in love with the Catalonian lifestyle and expanded my world view towards that of environmentalism, sustainability, recycling, cultural differences, and so much more. After graduation, I packed my Jeep up with all that would fit and moved to Colorado.
