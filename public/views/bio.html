Vince Vahala

Hello! I'm Vince Vahala. Frontend Application Developer, UI/UX guru, CSS Ninja with a profound appreciation and childlike infatuation with mountains. Climbing, hiking, camping, and exploring in my free time. Bet I'll beat you to the summit.



From a small industrial town in Indiana, I grew up playing any sport imaginable in traveling leagues, in the backyard, or on a friend's outdoor baskeball hoop. I attended Indiana University in Beautiful Bloomington, IN, studying Environmental Management at IU's School of Public and Environmental Affairs as well as Marketing through the Kelley School of Business. While at school I found the opportunity to study abroad a semester in Barcelona, Catalonia, Espana where I fell in love with the Catalonian lifestyle. After graduation, I packed my Jeep up with all that would fit and moved to Colorado. Best decision of my life.
