  • whatshotSpoilers! PodcastcodeSee App

    Spoilers! is a movie podcast that reviews new and old film.

    Good, bad, or ugly, we discuss the plot, rate the movie (pasteurized or spoiled) and end each show with a trivia game. Our “Movie Review!” pods are a continuous game, where the winner of last week’s show gets to host a full length episode on the movie of their choice. Our minor episodes feature one-off criticisms of new movies and other media. WARNING – this podcast does not care about “spoiler alerts” and the show will unapologetically ruin movies for you. With that being said – sit back, relax, and let us ruin a film for you. This is Spoilers!

    Built using JavaScript, AngularJS, Node.js, and Google Material Design practices all as an individual project.

  • videogame_assetBeta ClimbrcodeSee App

    Beta... climbing jargon that designates information about a climb. In rock climbing this may include information about a climb's difficulty, crux, style, length, quality of rock, ease to protect, required equipment, and specific information about hand or foot holds.

    Beta Climbr is an infinite-scroller game where the main character, "Cliff C. Hanger", dodges falling rocks, eats Cliff Bars, collects ice axes and rope powerups, all while in pursuits of that once in a lifetime climb.

    Built using Unity3D Engine with C# Scripting; this game has 17 different C# scripts interacting simultaneously to get Cliff C. Hanger to the summit he's always dreamed of.

    "Mountains are not fair or unfair, they are just dangerous." - Reinhold Messner, Italian mountaineer, adventurer, explorer.
